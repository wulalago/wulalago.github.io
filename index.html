Research

My area of expertise is in artificial intelligence (AI) for in-silico clinical trials. Specifically, I focus on developing fundamental techniques for medical image digitization (e.g., segmentation/registration/meshing), as well as on the generative AI for virtual patient synthesis and virtual treatment simulation. Please see the following projects for more details.
