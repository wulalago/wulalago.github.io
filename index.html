I am a Ph.D. student in CISTIB at School of Computing, University of Leeds, supervised by Prof. Alejandro Frangi and Dr. Nishant Ravikumar.

I received my M.E. in MUSIC Lab at School of Biomedical Engineering, Health Science Center, Shenzhen University, supervised by Prof. Dong Ni. Research

My research interest covers deep learning in medical image analysis. Specifically, I am dedicated to 1) geometric generative models, 2) ultrasound analysis, and 3) robust learning in medical image analysis.

Please be free to contact me if you would like to collaborate with me!
