Haoran Dou (窦浩然)

I am a research associate at Department of Computer Science, University of Manchester, working with Prof. Alejandro F. Frangi.

I received my Ph.D. from the School of Computing at the University of Leeds, under the supervision of Alejandro F. Frangi and Nishant Ravikumar. Prior to that, I earned my M.E. from Shenzhen University, where I was mentored by Prof. Dong Ni. During my master's studies, I had the opportunity to visit the Computational Radiology Laboratory (CRL) at Boston Children's Hospital, working under the guidance of Dr. Ali Gholipour and Dr. Davood Karimi. Before embarking on my postgraduate journey, I completed my B.E. at Sichuan University.

Email  /  Google Scholar  /  GitHub  /  ResearchGate  /  LinkedIn

Research

My area of expertise is in artificial intelligence (AI) for in-silico clinical trials. Specifically, I focus on fundamental techniques for medical digitization, as well as the generative AI for virtual patient synthesis and treatment.
