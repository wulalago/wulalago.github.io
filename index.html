Research

My research interests cover machine learning in medical image computing. Specifically, I am dedicated to 1) geometric generative models for cardiology, 2) standard plane localization in prenatal analysis, 3) deep learning in medical image segmentation and 4) domain adaptation in clinical dataset.

Please be free to contact me if you would like to collaborate with me!
